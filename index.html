Quantum Computing Researcher

Hi! I'm Adam. I graduated with a Master's in Maths from Cambridge last year (2022) and am looking forward to making applications to PhD programmes in quantum computing in the autumn. I currently work remotely at the Hon Hai Quantum Computing Research Centre under Dr. Min-Hsiu Hsieh and with various collaborators investigating constructions of quantum locally testable codes.

Last summer, I completed a project with Dr. Sergii Strelchuk in Cambridge funded by the Royal Society focused on the Schur transform and generalised coupling. I look forward to returning to Cambridge this year to build on this work with Dr. Strelchuk and other collaborators, while continuing my work with the Hon Hai Research Institute.

When I'm not doing maths, I enjoy a lot of outdoor activities, particularly climbing and hiking, when my body permits me.
