Quantum Computing Researcher

Hi! I'm Adam. I graduated with a Master's in Maths from Cambridge last year (2022) and am looking forward to making applications to PhD programmes in quantum computing in the autumn, to begin in 2024. I currently work remotely at the Hon Hai Quantum Computing Research Centre under Dr. Min-Hsiu Hsieh and with various collaborators investigating qLDPC codes, in particular quantum locally testable codes. I have also recently been working with Dr. Sergii Strelchuk in Cambridge on various aspects of symmetry in QC, particularly the Schur transform and port-based teleportation.

My papers can be found here. I'll be attending the "QEC Meets HDX" workshop at the Weizmann Institute in January, as well as QIP 2024. Please come and say hi if you're at either of these.

When I'm not researching, I enjoy a lot of outdoor activities, particularly climbing and hiking, when my body permits me.
