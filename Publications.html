General Distance Balancing for Quantum Locally Testable Codes
View the Paper on arXiv
May 2023

Working with Ting-Chun Lin and Dr. Min-Hsiu Hsieh, we applied the general distance balancing construction due to Evra et al. to quantum locally testable codes, obtaining a lower bound on the soundness of the resultant code. By applying this to existing qLTC constructions, we obtain parameters in several new regimes. This paper has been accepted into AQIS 2023, where I will present it.

Generalised Coupling and an Elementary Algorithm for the Quantum Schur Transform
View the Paper on arXiv
May 2023

Dr. Sergii Strelchuk and I develop a new formalism for the study of the general coupling of quantum systems with SU(2) Clebsch-Gordan coefficients as a special case. We show classical simulability and efficient state preparation results in this formalism. From it, we discover a new and simple algorithm for the quantum Schur transform. This paper is under review by  Quantum .
